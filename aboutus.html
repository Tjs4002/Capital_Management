About Our Platform

What We Do

BudgetMS is a comprehensive enterprise platform designed to streamline capital allocation, asset tracking, and fund utilization across your entire organization. We provide real-time visibility, automated calculations, and consolidated reporting to help you manage your capital with confidence.

Centralized capital and asset management
Real-time fund allocation tracking
Secure user authentication and role-based access

Why BudgetMS?

We understand the complexity of managing capital across large organizations. Our platform eliminates spreadsheets and manual processes, replacing them with a single source of truth that everyone can trust.

Reduce errors and compliance risks
Improve decision-making with real-time insights
Save time and reduce operational overhead

Our Mission

To empower finance and operations teams with a reliable, intuitive platform that eliminates manual capital tracking, reduces errors, and enables decision-makers to see real-time visibility into fund allocation and asset utilization.

Our Vision

To become the go-to enterprise solution for capital governance — where every rupee is accounted for, every asset is tracked, and every decision is backed by accurate data and transparency.

Key Features

Capital Master

Manage capital by type, financial year, and fund allocation with automatic calculations.

Capital Request

Submit and track capital requests with requester info, quantities, and types.

Records List

View, search, filter, and export all records in a unified consolidated view.

Dashboard

At-a-glance statistics and real-time insights into capital utilization.

Secure Auth

Role-based access with secure authentication for data safety.

CSV Export

Export any filtered view to CSV for reporting and analytics.
